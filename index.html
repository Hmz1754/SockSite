Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes do you offer?

We offer sizes S (6-8), M (9-11), and L (12-14) for most of our socks. Some specialty socks come in one-size-fits-most.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available for an additional fee.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn socks in original packaging. Just contact us for a return label!

Are your socks machine washable?

Yes! All our socks are machine washable. We recommend washing in cold water and air drying for best results.

How do I join the Sock of the Month Club?

Click the "Sock of the Month Club" link in our promo banner at the top of the page to sign up for exclusive monthly socks!
